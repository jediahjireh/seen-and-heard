ABOUT US

At Seen & Heard, passion meets purpose in the fight against human-trafficking. With fierce determination, we aim to raise awareness, ignite change and provide support to survivors of this abhorrent crime. The heart of our organisation beats so that every survivor knows that they are not alone.

OUR MISSION

Burning brightly to illuminate the darkness of human-trafficking, we are warriors of hope and committed to breaking the chains of exploitation and aiding survivor empowerment. Our mission is not a statement but a call to action from the depths of our souls - a call to find, retrieve and empower survivors.

For survivors

You have a voice. You are not a victim, you are a victor. You are Seen & Heard! Resiliently reclaim your life and empower yourself.

For survivor supporters

Let's strive to educate ourselves to recognise the signs of trafficking and how we can take action responsibly. We need to educate our communities so that all are ready and able to provide support services to survivors of human-trafficking.

What do we do?

  • We create awareness:

    • We aspire to ignite the flames of awareness that spread like wildfire, lighting up the hidden corners in which trafficking lurks, through various platforms to educate the public about the realities of human-trafficking and how they can help prevent it, while eradicating belief in the stereotypes or romanticising of this heinous crime that is traditionally portrayed through TV shows and fictional novels. Every voice must be heard, every life must be valued and every soul must be free.

  • We advocate for justice:

    • We stand together to wield our voices to demand accountability and be catalysts for change. Fighting daily for a world where traffickers face swift and severe consequences through stronger laws and better protections, we work to protect the rights of survivors in every corner of the world.

  • We provide support services:

    • Survivors have access to various resources, namely: hotlines, shelter, health care, legal aid, protection, counselling and vocational training. We walk alongside those ensnared by trafficking's grasp with open arms - survivors are seen and heard! Collaboration with law enforcement agencies, NGOs, essential workers, businesses and other contributors will allow for panoptic anti-trafficking strategies to be developed.

Get involved

Let's be the beacon of hope in a valley of darkness. Join us in our relentless pursuit of freedom, safety and justice for all. We are stronger together - a force to be reckoned with!
